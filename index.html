About Us

Infinnovation reaches its third season. This time it will be better. bigger and more challenging as we invite all those who can indulge in this amalgamation of information and innovation. The current economic strata of India will be the basis to test the knowledge and creativity of the empowering young minds. If you have the skill, go for the kill. See you at Infinnovation.
